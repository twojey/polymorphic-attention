# Partie 1 — Mathematical Signatures of Attention Across Domains

> **Type** : article empirique de référence + benchmark.
> **Public** : community NeurIPS / ICLR / TMLR.
> **Statut** : indépendant du résultat ASP, publiable dès Sprint C + S5-S7 terminés.

## Abstract (draft)

We introduce a comprehensive battery of 98+ mathematical properties to characterize the algorithmic structure of attention matrices across 5+ domains (text, code, vision, structured biology, controlled SMNIST). For each domain Oracle, we measure 23 families of properties spanning spectral, structural, algebraic, dynamic, topological, hierarchical, and functional-analytic invariants. The catalog, freely available, is designed to identify the **minimal indistinguishability class** of an attention operator — i.e., the structural signature that determines whether (and how) it admits a sub-quadratic implementation. We pre-register 35 predictions and confront them with measured values, producing the first systematic cross-domain signature table.

## 1. Introduction

- Context : attention O(N²) is a fundamental scaling bottleneck.
- Existing efficient attention (Linformer, Performer, Longformer, Monarch, Butterfly) makes structural assumptions implicitly.
- Open question : **what is the actual structure** of attention learned by SOTA models? Empirical answer is lacking.
- Our contribution : a falsifiable, exhaustive benchmark + first cross-domain measurement.

## 3. The Catalog

### 3.1 Properties (98+ in 23 families)
- A spectral, B structural, C token statistics, D geometric, E information-theoretic, F dynamic, G algebraic, H cross-layer, I cross-head, J Markov, K topological, L frequency, M conditional, N Oracle-student, O displacement, P realization, Q hierarchical, R RKHS, S tensors, T equivariance, U sparse-structured, V analytic operators, W logical complexity.

### 3.2 Oracle selection
- OR (SMNIST controlled, ω/Δ/ℋ ground-truth)
- LL (Llama-3.2-1B, text)
- SC (StarCoder-2-3B, code)
- DV (DINOv2-base, vision)
- CL (CLIP-ViT-L/14, multimodal)
- ES (ESM-2 35M, biology)

### 3.3 Experimental protocol
- N régimes × N exemples par régime → AttentionDump FP64
- Battery executable parallel, MLflow logging
- Stress sweep paramétré par (ω, Δ, ℋ) ou équivalent
